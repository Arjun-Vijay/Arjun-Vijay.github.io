About Me

My name is Arjun Vijay and I'm an undergraduate student at The George Washington University curretly pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Minor in Mind-Brain studies. While I enjoy learning about the many facets of computer science I am very interested in studying learning patterns of the human brain and understanding how I, as an aspiring computer scientist can work to model and replicate those functions within technology.

During my time at GWU, I've had the oppurunity to be involved with a number of wonderful organizations and programs. In addition being a named a Clark Engineering Scholar and GW Presidential Scholar, I've been involved with several on-campus organizations including Hackital, Atoms to Astronauts, and the GW Chess Club. To learn more about me or my experiences, feel free to to leave a commnt or reach out to me on social media.
